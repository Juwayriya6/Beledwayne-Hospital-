About the Project

Community-led care in Hirshabelle

Start with the ward that can save the most lives

This project begins with donated land and a clear first goal: building a mother, newborn, and child clinic before expanding further.

Our mission

To create a trusted place for safer pregnancy care, dignified childbirth, early newborn support, and pediatric treatment for underserved families in Hirshabelle. The goal is a realistic and transparent build that donors can understand and help bring to life.

Our story

Your aunt donated the land so it could serve the community. That act of generosity created the foundation for a phased health project: Phase 1 focuses on mothers and children first, then later phases can expand into emergency care, broader services, and community infrastructure.
