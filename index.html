About the Project

A donated piece of land can become a life-saving center

This project begins with a generous act: land donated by your aunt to serve people in Hirshabelle. The vision is to create a trusted place where mothers can receive safer care during pregnancy and childbirth, newborns can be treated earlier, and children can access essential medical support closer to home.

We are starting with the most urgent and realistic first step: a mother-and-child-focused clinic. As support grows, the project can expand into broader emergency and community health services.

Phase 1 Focus

Why donors care

Donors are not just paying for a building. They are helping create a trusted place where a mother can arrive before complications become fatal and where a child can receive treatment before illness becomes severe.
