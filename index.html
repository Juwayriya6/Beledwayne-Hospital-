Our Story

A donated piece of land can become a place that saves lives

This project began with a meaningful act of generosity: land in Beledweyne was donated so it could serve the surrounding community. The vision is to build a healthcare space that gives vulnerable families access to safe maternity care, newborn support, and treatment for children.

Rather than trying to promise a full hospital immediately, we are starting with the most urgent need first: a strong mother and child clinic. This makes the project more realistic, more transparent, and more fundable.

Why start with maternity and children?

When mothers do not have access to skilled care during pregnancy and childbirth, and when newborns and children cannot access timely care, the consequences are often fatal. A focused mother-and-child clinic can address some of the most urgent and preventable causes of death.

  • Safer antenatal and postnatal care
  • Cleaner and safer deliveries
  • Faster newborn assessment and stabilization
  • Pediatric treatment for common illnesses
  • Basic nutrition and medication support

What donors are really funding

Donors are not only funding a building. They are helping create a trusted place where a pregnant woman can seek help before labor, where a newborn can receive urgent early care, and where a sick child can be treated before it is too late.

This is why the first phase should stay tightly focused on the maternity and child ward.

Delivery Room

Clean, dignified childbirth spaces with trained attendants and essential supplies.

Newborn Corner

Warmth, early newborn assessment, infection prevention, and basic stabilization.

Pediatric Room

Treatment for fever, dehydration, infection, diarrhea, and respiratory illness.

Nutrition Support

Basic child nutrition screening and care for vulnerable infants and young children.

Pharmacy & Lab

Essential medicines and simple tests to support maternity and child care.
