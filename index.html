Why this project matters

A place where mothers and children can reach care sooner

Start with the highest-impact ward first

This campaign is centered on a realistic first build: a mother, newborn, and child clinic that can later grow into a wider health campus.

Phase 1 is designed to fund a delivery room, newborn corner, pediatric consultation room, basic pharmacy, simple diagnostics, and essential water and power setup.

By focusing on maternity and child survival first, donors can clearly see what their support helps make possible.
